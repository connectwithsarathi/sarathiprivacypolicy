TRANSMISSION

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with advertisers. We utilize specific third-party infrastructure only for essential service operations:

Telegram API: All license "Usage Pings" are transmitted via an encrypted administrative bot. This allows us to monitor license status and remaining days in real-time.

  • Security: Preventing unauthorized multi-device license use.

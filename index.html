1. Introduction

Welcome to Sarathi. We value your privacy and believe in complete transparency. This policy outlines how we handle your data to ensure you have a safe and secure experience.

2. Your Data, Your Control

Sarathi is designed to be a private tool.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or monitor your personal information, such as your name, email, or usage habits.
  • Local Storage: Everything you enter into the app stays on your device. We (the developers) have no way of seeing or accessing your records.

3. Cloud Backup Feature

Private Ecosystem

To help you keep your data safe, Sarathi offers an optional backup feature using Google Drive.

Limited Access: When you enable backups, the app is only allowed to access a specific folder it creates for Sarathi. It cannot see or touch your personal photos, documents, or any other files in your Google account.

Private Storage: Your backup files are stored directly in your own Google account. We do not have a separate server, so your data never leaves your personal ecosystem.

4. Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Device Security: We use standard mobile security protocols to keep your information isolated from other apps.
  • Encryption: When you choose to back up your data, it is protected by Google’s high-level security and encryption during the transfer and while stored in the cloud.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with anyone. There are no hidden trackers or advertising tools inside the app.

6. Managing Your Data

You are in full control of your information:

  • Deleting Data: You can delete your local data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • Deleting Backups: You can remove your cloud backups by deleting the backup folder directly from your Google Drive.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.

Data Architecture

Data Collection & Transparency

Sarathi is designed with an Offline-First philosophy. Your business data stays where it belongs: on your device.
  • Financial Ledger Content: Customer names, balances, and transaction history. (Stored locally; we have no access).
  • Identity & License Data: Business Name, PAN Number, and License Expiry. (Used for license management and support).
  • Security Identifiers: Unique Device ID for hardware license locking.
Note on Personal/Sensitive Data: Some data we collect, such as PAN numbers and hardware-bound identifiers, is considered personal information. This is used strictly for authentication purposes.
Privacy Commitment

We do not have access to your individual financial records. Our administrative systems only see aggregate licensing metadata to verify activation status.

Transmission & Sharing

Security Protocols

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services. Administrative API: The app communicates via encrypted TLS/SSL protocols to monitor license activation. We do not share your personal data with third parties except where required by law.
User Rights

Your Control Over Data

You have the right to:
  • Access & Correction: Request a copy of your registration records.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your account metadata from our licensing bot.
  • Children’s Privacy: This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
Objectives

Retention Policy

We retain licensing metadata only for the duration of your active subscription. Once a license is canceled or expires, metadata is purged from our systems according to our maintenance schedule.

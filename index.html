Privacy Policy for Sarathi
Last Updated: April 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Sarathi ("we," "our," or "the app") handles user information. We value your privacy and only access or collect data necessary for the app's licensing and functional operations.

1. Information Collected & Transmitted

Device Identifiers: The app reads your device's unique Android ID to tie your license to your hardware. Functional Telemetry: To monitor app health and license validity, the app automatically sends a connection ping to our private administrative channel via a secure, encrypted HTTPS connection. This ping contains your Registration ID/PAN, license expiry status, and your total count of records (Total Parties and Transactions).
IMPORTANT: Sarathi does NOT collect or transmit the sensitive content of your ledger. Your customer names, phone numbers, and individual transaction amounts remain 100% offline on your device.

2. Manual Data Collection

Information is collected manually when you choose to provide it for licensing via WhatsApp or Email: Identification: System Registration ID and PAN Number. Contact Information: Email Address and Mobile Number used for sending license keys and critical updates.

3. Purpose of Data Use

We use the collected information solely for: App Functionality: Verifying your license and providing offline service. Service Monitoring: Ensuring the app is connecting properly and detecting license fraud. Communication: Sending license keys and notifying you of policy changes.

4. Policy Changes & Notifications

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify registered users who have provided their contact information via: Email or Mobile Number (WhatsApp/SMS), based on the contact method you provided during registration. We also recommend users check the "Privacy Policy" section in the app periodically for the latest version of this policy.

5. Data Sharing and Security

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your identifiers, PAN numbers, emails, or phone numbers with third-party companies or advertisers. Security: All data received manually is stored securely in our private records and is handled with strict confidentiality.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at: Email: connectwithsarathi@gmail.com
